📊 OP.GG Stats Card
v1.2.0
✅ Active on OP.GG
Automatically displays a draggable stats card on OP.GG summoner pages with detailed game statistics and analytics.

✨ Features:

  • 📈 Total playtime and games count
  • 🏆 Most played champion statistics
  • ⚔️ Laning win rate analysis
  • 🥇 Average ranking performance
  • 🔄 Auto-scroll to load more games
  • 📱 Draggable and collapsible interface
  • 💾 Settings saved across sessions
